We share the information you submit with third-party companies, lead buyers, attorneys, and service providers who may be able to help with your solar contract, and with vendors that support our operations (such as hosting, analytics, and call-routing providers). We retain your information for as long as needed to fulfill the purposes described here and to meet legal requirements. We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your data, though no method of transmission is completely secure.
