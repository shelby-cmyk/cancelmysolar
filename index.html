How solar companies let homeowners down

If any of this sounds familiar, you may have grounds to act.

Misleading sales pitch

You were promised "free" panels or that the system would eliminate your electric bill — and the numbers never matched reality.

Payments that keep rising

An escalator clause is quietly increasing your monthly payment every year — on top of the electric bill you still pay.

Underperforming panels

The system produces far less than you were told, or it's been broken for months while you keep paying.

A lien blocking your sale

A UCC filing or lien tied to your panels is making it hard — or impossible — to sell or refinance your home.

Your installer vanished

Over 100 solar companies have gone bankrupt since 2023 — leaving warranties worthless and no one to call.

Never finished or permitted

Installation stalled, was never inspected, or never got permission to operate — yet the payments started anyway.
